About OMC (OM Console)

Built on the Oracle quoting application (constituent of Oracle Enterprise Business suite), this system is designed to receive orders progressed through Ascend-QuoteEdge from the sales ops /support teams that progress the quotes after receiving the required attachments. Typically, all orders received here are revenue impacting.

The Order Management Helpdesk provides global order support for our internal customers and partners, and is manned by various OM members throughout the geos.

Norway
  • Common INCOTERMS: EXW, FCA, CPT
  • VAT Rate: 25% standard rate
  • Special Notes: Customs documentation must include EORI number
  • Lead Time: 3-5 business days for standard shipments
Turkey
  • Common INCOTERMS: DAP, DDP, FOB
  • VAT Rate: 18% standard rate
  • Special Notes: Requires commercial invoice in Turkish
  • Lead Time: 5-7 business days for standard shipments
